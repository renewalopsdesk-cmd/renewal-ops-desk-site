CONTRACT RENEWAL BRIEF

CTR-004 · Enterprise Creative Suite

Critical | Deadline: May 10, 2026 | Value: $50,811.60/year

Situation

CTR-004 is a creative software agreement used across production teams. The agreement renews automatically for another 12 months unless non-renewal notice is served before the deadline. The renewal also triggers a fixed 15% annual price increase.

Top risks

  • Automatic renewal: inaction can lock the company into another 12 months at $50,811.60.
  • Fixed 15% escalation: Year 4 increases by $6,627.60 without further approval.
  • No seat reductions: unused seats cannot be removed during the term.

Clause vs market standard

Contract

Annual fee increases by a fixed 15% upon renewal without further acceptance.

Market standard

Enterprise SaaS increases commonly sit in the 3–7% range, usually with a cap or inflation reference.

Worst-case exposure

Base renewal value$50,811.60
Year 5 projected value$58,433.34
Two-year value vs current price$20,876.94

What to do

  1. Confirm whether non-renewal notice was served before May 10, 2026.
  2. Ask vendor to cap renewal escalation at 5% or CPI-linked with a maximum ceiling.
  3. Request annual seat flexibility before signing any renewal order.
