FAQ

Before you submit your agreements.

Is this legal advice?

No. Renewal Ops Desk provides commercial and operational contract visibility. We do not provide legal advice, legal review, contract drafting, or legal representation.

Do you contact vendors?

No. We do not contact suppliers, vendors, counterparties, or external parties on your behalf.

What happens after payment?

You are redirected to onboarding, where you submit company information and upload or link the agreements for review.

Who reviews the agreements?

Renewal Ops Desk reviews agreements under a commercial and operational scope focused on renewal exposure, notice windows, escalation clauses, and portfolio visibility.

How are documents handled?

Documents are handled through structured intake and controlled access. They are used only to produce the requested commercial review.

When are files deleted?

Contract documents are deleted from active systems within 15 days after delivery, subject to limited exceptions described in the Data Policy.

Do we need calls or meetings?

No. The process is async by default: checkout, onboarding, document submission, report delivery.

What contract types are accepted?

Service agreements, MSAs, SOWs, order forms, pricing schedules, renewal notices, amendments, addenda, supplier emails, and vendor lists may be submitted when relevant.
