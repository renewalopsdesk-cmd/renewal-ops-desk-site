FAQ

Questions before the scan.

Is Renewal Ops Desk legal advice?

No. Renewal Ops Desk provides commercial analysis of existing vendor agreements. It does not provide legal advice, legal opinions, contract drafting, or legal representation.

Do you negotiate with vendors for us?

No. The report gives your team a negotiation playbook. Your team handles the vendor conversation.

Do we need to book a call?

No. The process is fully async. No calls are required at any stage.

When does the 2-business-day delivery window start?

The delivery window starts only after we receive the complete set of required contracts and onboarding information.

Do we need to submit all contracts at once?

For Setup Only, yes. For Setup + Monthly, the initial portfolio is submitted during onboarding and new contracts can be added throughout the month within plan limits.

Is this a CLM platform?

No. Renewal Ops Desk is a contract risk intelligence service focused on active vendor agreements.
