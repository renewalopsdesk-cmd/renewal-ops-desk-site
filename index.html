About Renewal Ops Desk

A commercial review desk for contracts nobody is actively watching.

Renewal Ops Desk reviews active vendor agreements and turns them into clear commercial visibility: renewal dates, pricing escalations, notice windows, unused spend, and agreements that need action.

FAQ

Before you submit your agreements.

Who actually reviews our contracts?

Renewal Ops Desk reviews agreements under a commercial and operational scope focused on renewal exposure, notice windows, escalation clauses, and portfolio visibility.

Is this confidential?

Yes. We do not contact vendors, publish client findings, or share your documents externally. Contract documents are deleted from active systems within 15 days after delivery, subject to limited exceptions in the Data Policy.

Is this legal advice?

No. Renewal Ops Desk does not provide legal advice, legal review, contract drafting, legal opinions, or legal representation.

What happens after payment?

You are redirected to onboarding, where you submit company information and upload or link the agreements for review.

Do you contact vendors?

No. We do not contact suppliers, vendors, counterparties, or external parties on your behalf.

Do we need calls or meetings?

No. The process is async by default: checkout, onboarding, document submission, report delivery.
