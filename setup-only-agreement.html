Renewal Ops Desk

Setup Only consists of a one-time portfolio analysis with delivery of a structured executive report containing portfolio visibility, identified commercial risks, potential financial exposure, critical deadlines, negotiation opportunities, and commercial recommendations.

The service is commercial, operational, administrative, analytical, financial, and strategic in nature. Renewal Ops Desk is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice, legal opinions, contract drafting, representation, or negotiation with suppliers.

The client must submit all applicable contracts and documents in one single organized submission, through the indicated channel. Documents submitted later may be disregarded or treated as an additional service.

Documents must be complete, legible, accessible, current, and usable. Artificially merged contracts, incomplete documents, corrupted files, low-quality scans, password-protected documents without password, and disorganized files may be rejected or pause the timeline.

The standard delivery timeline is up to 5 business days after complete receipt of usable documents, unless otherwise stated in the proposal or agreement.

The client remains solely responsible for decisions to negotiate, renew, cancel, sign, amend, or terminate any contract.

The client may upgrade from Setup Only to Setup + Monthly within 14 calendar days after delivery without paying a second setup fee, subject to the same entity and same analyzed portfolio.
