Renewal Ops Desk

Renewal Ops Desk provides commercial, operational, and organizational contract support for businesses that want better visibility over their contracts, renewal dates, financial exposure, and negotiation opportunities.

Our services may include contract portfolio setup, contract organization, renewal calendar creation, deadline tracking, commercial risk identification, financial exposure estimates, renewal reports, and ongoing contract monitoring, depending on the plan selected.

All services are provided under a separate service agreement or commercial order form, which defines the applicable plan, tier, pricing, scope, delivery timeline, included contract volume, and any additional terms.

Renewal Ops Desk does not provide legal advice, legal opinions, legal review, contract drafting, representation, or negotiation with third parties. Our work is strictly commercial, operational, administrative, analytical, financial, and strategic.

Clients are responsible for submitting complete, updated, legible, and organized documents. Contracts that are incomplete, unreadable, corrupted, artificially merged, outdated, or missing relevant attachments may be rejected, returned, or excluded from the analysis until corrected.

Delivery timelines begin only after we receive documents in a complete, accessible, and usable format. Standard delivery for initial setup is up to 5 business days, unless otherwise stated in the applicable agreement or proposal.

All recommendations, reports, benchmarks, estimates, and alerts are intended to support internal business decisions. The client remains solely responsible for deciding whether to renew, cancel, negotiate, sign, amend, or terminate any contract.

In case of any conflict between this website and the signed service agreement, the signed service agreement will prevail.
