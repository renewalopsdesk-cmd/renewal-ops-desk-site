Renewal Ops Desk

Setup + Monthly consists of portfolio mapping setup, monthly monitoring, renewal reports before critical deadlines, new contract intake within tier limits, and quarterly portfolio review.

The service is commercial, operational, administrative, analytical, financial, and strategic in nature. Renewal Ops Desk does not provide legal advice, legal opinions, contract drafting, legal representation, or direct negotiation with suppliers.

The client submits all active contracts in one organized initial submission for setup. New contracts, renewals, amendments, notices, renewal proposals, supplier communications, and relevant updates must be proactively submitted by the client after setup.

Renewal Ops Desk does not monitor the client’s internal systems, email inboxes, supplier portals, invoices, proposals, or communications not provided by the client.

Individual reports are generally prepared before critical deadlines, depending on timely, complete, correct, and usable submission of documents.

Monthly new contract limits apply by tier. Unused slots and credits do not roll over, transfer, generate credit, discount, refund, or future compensation.

Packs and Expansion Packs may be purchased for additional capacity. Recurring use may trigger commercial review of the plan, tier, SLA, price, or scope.

After cancellation, contract documents are deleted from active systems within 15 days, subject to limited retention for legal, tax, accounting, security, dispute, backup, or rights-defense purposes.

No calls. Clear report. 2 business days after complete submission.
