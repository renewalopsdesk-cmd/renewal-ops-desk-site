Renewal Ops Desk

At Renewal Ops Desk, we handle your contracts with care, confidentiality, and purpose.

We only use the documents and information you share with us to provide the service you hired: organizing your contracts, identifying key dates, tracking renewal deadlines, preparing commercial reports, and helping you maintain visibility over your contract portfolio.

We do not sell your data.

We do not use your contracts for unrelated purposes.

We do not publicly share your information.

We do not provide legal advice or legal review.

Your documents may be processed through secure tools, software, cloud storage, automation systems, artificial intelligence tools, or trusted service providers that help us deliver the service efficiently. When we use third-party tools or providers, we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your information and keep it confidential.

For Setup Only, contract documents are removed from our active systems within 15 days after delivery of the final report, unless limited retention is necessary for legal, tax, accounting, payment, security, dispute, backup, or rights-defense purposes.

For Setup + Monthly, your documents remain in our active systems while your plan is active, so we can monitor deadlines, maintain your contract calendar, and prepare ongoing reports. After cancellation, contract documents are removed from our active systems within 15 days, subject to limited legal, tax, security, dispute, or backup retention.

We may retain final reports, invoices, payment records, delivery confirmations, signed agreements, and essential service logs for administrative and compliance purposes.

We may also use aggregated or anonymized information to improve our internal methods, benchmarks, and service quality — but never in a way that identifies your company, suppliers, contracts, or pricing.

Your contracts stay yours. Our role is to keep them organized, visible, and easier to manage.
