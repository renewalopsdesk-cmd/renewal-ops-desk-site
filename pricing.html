FAQ

Before you choose a plan.

What does the Setup + Monthly payment include?

It includes the initial portfolio setup and the first month monitoring fee. Future recurring monthly billing is activated separately after onboarding and setup completion.

Can we pay upfront annually?

Yes. Annual upfront monitoring links are available for Starter, Growth, and Guardian and include two months on us compared with paying monthly for 12 months.

What is the difference between Setup Only and Setup + Monthly?

Setup Only is a one-time audit of the current portfolio. Setup + Monthly includes initial setup and ongoing monitoring after onboarding, designed for teams that want renewal visibility to continue.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. Renewal Ops Desk provides commercial and operational contract analysis only. We do not provide legal advice, legal review, contract drafting, legal representation, or legal opinions.

Do you contact vendors?

No. Renewal Ops Desk does not contact vendors, negotiate on your behalf, send notices, or represent your company. The service gives your team visibility and recommended action areas.

What if we have more than 75 active contracts?

Enterprise review is available for larger portfolios. Email hello@renewalopsdesk.com and we can scope the right structure.
